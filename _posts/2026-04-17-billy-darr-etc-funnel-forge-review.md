## What is Funnel Forge?

In my analysis of its proposed architecture and feature set, I see it fitting squarely into the digital marketing tools category, specifically designed to simplify and accelerate the sales funnel creation process for online entrepreneurs. It's built for those who understand the critical importance of a well-structured sales funnel but are often deterred by the technical demands, the fragmented nature of combining multiple services, or the prohibitive costs associated with high-end solutions.
