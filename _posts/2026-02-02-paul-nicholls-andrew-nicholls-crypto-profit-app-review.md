The "advanced" aspect might imply machine learning algorithms that adapt to market conditions, or a wider array of sophisticated strategies than basic bots.
    *   **How it works:** A user would likely connect their exchange account (e.g., Binance, Coinbase Pro) via API keys. They would then select a strategy, define parameters like investment amount, risk tolerance, and perhaps specific cryptocurrencies to trade. The bot then takes over, executing trades without human intervention.

They could set up the automation feature, and the bot would continue to look for profit opportunities even while they are at work or asleep. If one asset performs exceptionally well, the bot might automatically sell a portion to buy into underperforming assets, maintaining a desired asset allocation and potentially maximizing long-term growth.
