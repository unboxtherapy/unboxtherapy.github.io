My testing revealed a system that allows for highly granular control over content distribution. *   **Drip Campaigns:** I could set up a sequence of posts to be released over days or weeks, perfect for product launches or educational series.

**Real-world applications:** Imagine an affiliate marketer promoting a new product. Instead of manually posting on five different platforms, adapting the message for each, and tracking engagement, Viral Blast handles it.
