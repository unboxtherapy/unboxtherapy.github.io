*   **Content Recognition:** Identifying objects, faces, speech, and key actions within clips. For example, if you upload footage of a cooking demonstration, the AI should be able to recognize ingredients being added, mixing actions, and the final plating.
*   **Emotional and Tonal Analysis:** While still nascent in AI, I anticipate the system will attempt to gauge the mood or energy of different segments, helping it select clips that align with a desired emotional arc for the final video.
