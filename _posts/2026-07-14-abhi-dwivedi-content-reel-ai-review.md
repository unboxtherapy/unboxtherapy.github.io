To accompany the generated scripts, a high-quality text-to-speech (TTS) engine is essential. I anticipate Content Reel AI will include an advanced AI voiceover module capable of converting scripts into natural-sounding narration.

*   **How it works:** Users would select from a library of diverse AI voices (male/female, various accents, tones) and the AI would automatically generate a voiceover for their script.
